Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help visitors understand the directory and status display.

How are the top websites chosen?

They are sorted by active status first, then stability score, then lower response time.

Why are some websites not clickable?

If a website is currently inactive, it is shown for reference only and cannot be opened.

Why are some websites hidden under Show More?

Only the top 3 available websites are shown first. Additional listings are grouped below.

How often is the page updated?

The website status list refreshes automatically every 30 minutes.
